It is a digital platform that connects artisans with buyers, offers e-learning, and preserves cultural heritage using modern technology.

Browse the marketplace, add products to your cart or wishlist, and proceed to secure checkout using various payment methods.

Artisans can register, create a profile, and upload their products. The admin team verifies and approves new artisan accounts.

Users can access tutorials, webinars, and artisan-led courses on craft skills, culture, and entrepreneurship.
